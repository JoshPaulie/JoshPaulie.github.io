Goon League of Legends Code of Conduct

Preamble

This Code of Conduct ("the Code") establishes the rules and regulations governing the conduct of all participants ("the Players") in League of Legends games played amongst friends ("the Games"). By participating in the Games, all Players agree to abide by the following provisions, which are considered legally binding.

Section 1: Prohibition of Passive Aggressive Comments

1.1 Players shall refrain from making any passive aggressive comments during the Games.

1.2 Passive aggressive comments include, but are not limited to, sarcastic remarks, sighing, veiled insults, "you're crazy", and criticism retroactively disguised as banter.

1.3 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and mandatory Speak-With-Your-Chest training.

Section 2: No Upset Regarding Jungle Lane Attendance

2.1 Players shall not express frustration (or harbor resentment) towards the jungler if they fail to attend their respective lane.

2.2 Each Player acknowledges that the jungler has the autonomy to prioritize their efforts as they see fit, without undue influence or coercion.

2.3 It is important to note that during the laning phase, it is the sole responsibility of the laner to play safe and exercise caution.

2.4 Laners should be mindful of their positioning, warding, and map awareness to mitigate the risks of potential ganks or enemy aggression.

2.5 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and being assigned to play as the jungle in the next game.

Section 3: Prohibition of Boasting About Won Lanes

3.1 Players shall refrain from mentioning their victory in a lane if the opposing lane opponent later becomes fed.

3.2 Players acknowledge that if they were truly ahead in their respective lane, they should have been able to carry the game independently.

3.3 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and mandatory 90-day humility training.

Section 4: Consensus-Based Play Ruination

4.1 Players shall not intentionally ruin a play that a majority of the team agrees with.

4.2 Ruining a play includes sabotaging team strategies, disregarding agreed-upon tactics, or intentionally hindering the team's progress.

4.3 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games, getting flamed, and being assigned the fill role for the next game.

Section 5: Bot Lane Dragon Priority Rotation

5.1 Bot lane players shall prioritize rotating to the dragon pit when they have lane priority and the jungle player deems it appropriate.

5.2 The jungler holds the authority to determine the timing and suitability of the dragon rotation.

5.3 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and being assigned to play as the support role in the next game.

Section 6: Restriction on Questioning Team Positioning and Activity

6.1 Players shall refrain from questioning their team's positioning or current activity/inactivity if they are caught out of position and subsequently eliminated by the enemy team.

6.2 Each Player acknowledges that individual mistakes and mispositioning can happen, and it is not the responsibility of the team to adjust their playstyle based on such occurrences.

6.3 Violators of this provision may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and being required to recite the phrase "I shall accept responsibility for my positioning mishaps and not question my team's decisions" before participating in the next game.

Section 7: Discouragement of One-Tricking and Encouragement of Team Composition

7.1 It is strongly discouraged for players to engage in one-tricking, focusing exclusively on playing a single champion throughout multiple games.

7.2 One-tricking can hinder team composition and limit strategic flexibility, potentially impacting the overall success of the team.

7.4 Due to this, the use of ridicule and intimidation is an acceptable approach to address fellow Players guilty of one-tricking.

7.5a Violators who DON'T engage in excessive ridicule or intimidation towards those who one-trick may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and required to undergo sportsmanship training.

7.5b Violators who engage in repeated one-tricking may be subject to penalties, including but not limited to, temporary exclusion from future Games and having their champion picked for them in the following game, ensuring a more diverse and team-oriented approach.

Section Z: Enforcement and Dispute Resolution

Z.1 The enforcement of this Code shall be overseen by Conrad, the designated "Code Arbiter" agreed upon by the Players.

Z.2 Disputes arising from the interpretation or application of this Code shall be resolved through friendly negotiations, compromising in the spirit of fair play.

Z.3 In the event of an unresolved dispute, Conrad shall have the final authority to determine the appropriate course of action.
